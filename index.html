So, who is who and what is he/she famous for

Lisa Crispin & Janet Gregory

  • Writers of “Agile Testing” and “More Agile Testing”
  • Had a talk on their latest book. Go read it!
  • The 'godmothers' of the conference

David Evans

  • Winner “favourite speaker 2013” with the talk Visualising Quality
  • This year “Pillars of Testing talk”
  • Partner with Gojko Adzic @Neuri Consulting (Spec by Example)
  • Watch his talk on Visualising Quality here

Huib Schoots

  • Context Driven & Exploratory Testing (workshop)
  • Loud guy, usually at FAT NL meetings
  • If you want to learn about these topics, go read his blog

Peter Walen

  • Liveblogging the conference for years
  • Did a workshop on Exploratory Testing that was super popular
  • Read his liveblog here

Newcomer for me: Antony Marcano

  • Keynote “Don’t put me in a box”
  • Biggest eyeopener for me that year
  • Read his blog here

Tom Roden & Ben Williams

  • Two young dudes with a fun personality
  • How does test management change with agile development?
  • Eric will tell you more about that!

Alan “The Evil Tester” Richardson

  • The ‘nerdy’ tester, had the closing keynote
  • Has a great online course on test automation with Selenium/Java
  • read more here
